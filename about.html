About Pateros Technological College

Pateros Technological College (PTC) envisions being a leading institution for holistic student development, producing globally competent graduates through quality, specialized instruction in tech and vocational fields, while fostering patriotism, strong morals, and environmental care; its mission involves providing accessible, skills-based education, enhancing creativity, and empowering marginalized individuals to contribute positively to society.

Vision

  • To be a leading institution committed to the holistic development of students for the betterment of their lives and society.
  • To foster creativity, leadership, and resourcefulness in students.
  • To produce graduates who are technologically adept, professionally competent, and socially responsible.

Mission

  • Provide Quality Education: Offer specialized professional instruction and training in scientific, technological, industrial, and vocational fields.
  • Develop Character: Enhance moral, spiritual, and cultural values, instilling patriotism and love for the country.
  • Promote Inclusivity: Offer educational opportunities, especially to marginalized individuals.
  • Foster Skills: Heighten students' creativity and leadership through co-curricular activities.
  • Champion the Environment: Promote environmental awareness and unconditional love for the Earth.
  • Support Graduates: Offer internship opportunities and possible employment pathways.

Core Values

Responsibility, Integrity, Compassion, Creativity, Commitment, Excellence, Environmental Concern.
