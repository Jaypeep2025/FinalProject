Admission Requirements

Welcome to the first step of your journey with PTC. We accept applications for the upcoming Academic Year 2025-2026 starting January 15, 2025.

Incoming Freshmen

  • Original Form 138 (Report Card)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • 2 pcs 2x2 ID Picture (White background)
  • Barangay Clearance
  • Long Brown Envelope

Transferees

  • Honorable Dismissal / Transfer Credential
  • Transcript of Records (TOR) for evaluation
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • 2 pcs 2x2 ID Picture
  • Entrance Examination Result

Admission Procedures

Submit application, present Form 138, take entrance exam (if required), and pay fees. Await evaluation and enrollment instructions from the Registrar.
Returning students should update their enrollment through the student portal or visit the Registrar to settle any academic or financial requirements.
Irregular students must submit academic records for evaluation and coordinate with the Registrar to determine transferable units and enrollment status.
Contact the Admissions Office for bespoke enrollment procedures for executive or special class programs, including schedules and fees.
