BS Information Technology

School of IT

The BSIT program focuses on developing students’ technical skills in software development, networking, database management, and cybersecurity. Graduates are prepared to design and implement IT solutions, manage systems, and contribute to the digital transformation of organizations.

BS Office Administration

School of Administration

BSOA equips students with managerial, administrative, and leadership skills needed to efficiently run offices and business operations. The program emphasizes office management, human resources, business communication, and organizational development.

Computer & Communication Systems (CCS)

2-Year Course

The CCS program provides foundational knowledge in computing, programming, and problem-solving. It is designed for students who want to start a career in IT or pursue further studies in computer science and related fields.

Certificate in Office Administration (COA)

2-Year Course

COA trains students in essential office and administrative tasks, including clerical work, record management, basic accounting, and communication skills. Graduates can efficiently support office operations in various industries.

Associate in Hotel & Restaurant Management (AHRHM)

2-Year Course

AHRHM prepares students for careers in the hospitality and tourism industry. It covers hotel operations, food and beverage management, customer service, and event planning, equipping graduates with practical skills to excel in hotels, restaurants, and tourism-related businesses.
